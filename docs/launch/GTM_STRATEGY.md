# Go-to-Market (GTM) Strategy

## 1. Executive Summary
The SageMaster Telegram Signal Copier is a strategic feature designed to increase user retention and attract new users who rely on Telegram signal providers. By offering a seamless, cloud-based integration directly into the SageMaster ecosystem, we eliminate the need for users to manage complex VPS setups or third-party EAs.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Primary**: Existing SageMaster Forex (SFX) users who follow Telegram signal channels but struggle with manual execution or the complexity of existing copiers.
- **Secondary**: Crypto traders using `app.sagemaster.io` who want to automate signals from crypto VIP groups.
- **Tertiary**: Signal providers themselves, who can recommend SageMaster as the easiest way for their followers to automate their signals.

## 3. Positioning & Messaging
- **Core Message**: "Automate your favorite Telegram signals directly into your SageMaster account. No VPS, no EAs, just paste your webhook and go."
- **Key Differentiator**: We are the *only* signal copier that natively integrates with SageMaster's advanced risk management and order routing infrastructure.
- **Brand Identity**: The product will be launched as a **standalone brand** with its own dashboard, separate from the core SageMaster UI, to maintain neutrality while deeply integrating via webhooks.

## 4. Pricing Strategy
The product operates on a standalone subscription model with annual billing options (2 months free). Plans can be toggled on/off by admins.

- **Free Tier**: 1 active destination, AI parsing, signal logs.
- **Starter ($29/mo)**: 2 active destinations, configurable notifications.
- **Pro ($59/mo)**: 5 active destinations, multiple TPs, trailing SL, auto-breakeven (V2 features).
- **Elite ($99/mo)**: 15 active destinations, provider commands, risk overrides, MT5 EA source (V2 features).

## 5. Launch Timeline
- **T-minus 4 Weeks**: Internal alpha testing with employee Telegram accounts.
- **T-minus 2 Weeks**: Closed beta with 50 highly active SageMaster users.
- **Launch Day**: Announcement via email newsletter, in-app banner, and a dedicated webinar demonstrating the setup process.
- **T-plus 2 Weeks**: Outreach to top 10 Telegram signal providers offering an affiliate partnership.

## 6. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Adoption**: Number of users who successfully connect a Telegram account.
- **Engagement**: Number of active channels configured per user.
- **Volume**: Total number of signals successfully parsed and routed per week.
- **Reliability**: Webhook delivery success rate (>99%).
